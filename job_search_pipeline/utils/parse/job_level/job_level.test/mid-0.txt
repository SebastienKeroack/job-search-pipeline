About Custom Management Solutions

Custom Management Solutions Inc. is an Alberta-based company that bridges **construction management, property operations, and technology**. We build practical, production-ready software systems that support real-world workflows across **real estate, property management, and construction**.

Our technology stack includes **Power Apps for internal users**, **Dataverse as a centralized data layer**, and **custom web applications** that provide simple, accessible interfaces for external clients. Our focus is on reliability, maintainability, and solving real operational problems—not experimental or hype-driven software.

As our client-facing systems continue to grow, we are expanding our development capacity and looking for a **Web Developer** who can support active projects during **daytime business hours**.

The Role
